Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Momentum ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes your nickname that you provide when using the App.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including tasks, challenges, and other activity data you create within the App.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device, such as device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers needed for the App to function properly.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App functionality
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Personalize your experience
  • Respond to your requests or questions
  • Develop new features and services
  • Debug and fix issues in the App

Data Storage and Sync

Momentum offers cloud synchronization to keep your data consistent across multiple devices. Your data is:

  • Stored securely in the cloud
  • Transmitted using encryption
  • Associated with your user profile

You can use the cloud sync feature without requiring sign-in with third-party services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data for analytical purposes.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Update your nickname at any time by clicking on your name in the App
  • Choose whether to enable or disable cloud synchronization
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
